Mission Four: Wikipedia, A Water Cooler For Digital Zombies

Now that you've gotten a chance to sift through sources that Digital Zombies haven't infected, the mysterious person wants you to look at one of the most frequently-used, Digital-Zombified parts of the Internet -- Wikipedia.

You stare at the mysterious person, eyes wide. Really? Wikipedia? Are you sure that's a good idea?

Of course it's a good idea, the mysterious person tells you. Not all of the information on Wikipedia is bad, after all. You just need to learn how to distinguish the good from the bad.

The mysterious person then shoves you out the door and sends you on your way to find a computer. Wikipedia it is, then, you guess.
