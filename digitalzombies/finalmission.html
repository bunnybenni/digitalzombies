Mission 10: Your Final Report

The mysterious person is weeping with joy over how far you've come since you started. (Look, there's another emotion that isn't "pleased!") You're almost ready, they say, you just have one final task.

Now you're going to take that draft you wrote during your last mission and incorporate the feedback that you got from that random non-Digital Zombie. Polish it until it sparkles -- make everyone believe it!

You're pushed out the door to go on your final mission. For once, you feel ready. You're not sure whether you're ready for the mission or just to get the mysterious person out of your hair, but you're ready for something.

Here Are Your Final Instructions. Get Ready.
