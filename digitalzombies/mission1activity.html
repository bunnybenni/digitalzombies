Survey Your Surroundings

Your goal: Learn some basic mapping skills to survive the digital zombie infection.

Use the Internet (yes, it's okay, really) to find:

  • A map of the University of Oregon campus
  • A list of all of the libraries at the University of Oregon (including the off-campus libraries)

Print it out, and take it with you on a walk around campus. Take a notebook and a writing utensil with you. Visit all the libraries for at least 5-10 minutes per library, and jot down a thing or two that you found most interesting at each library.

Additionally, if a library has its own map of the building, get a copy. You'll thank me later.

Now It's Time to Report Your Findings!
