Mission 9: Lie To Me (Over And Over Again)

You bring your newly-fabricated evidence over to the mysterious person and they appear to be thrilled. You're stunned -- you didn't realize that the mysterious person had emotions other than "pleased."

Now the mysterious person wants you to start crafting your lie. This mission is just a draft, and you will be getting feedback from a mystery source (A mystery source? You mean this weird person has been having more than one of you go on all these missions? What's their endgame?).

You get sent off back to the library to start writing in a nice, quiet location. You may hear a Digital Zombie or two outside the window, but there's no need to worry -- they can't possibly enter the library...
